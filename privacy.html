Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy describes how Hypercritical Studio ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide to us when you use our website, products, or services.
1. Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information: When you use our website or engage our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information.
1.2 Usage Data: We may also collect non-personal information about your interactions with our website like referring website. We do not sell any of your information at any point of time. Ever!.
2. Use of Information
2.1 Service Delivery: We use your personal information to provide the services you request, such as web design or app development.
2.2 Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about our services, promotions, or important updates.
Improvement: We may use usage data to analyze trends, improve our website, and enhance user experience.
3. Data Security
3.1 Protection: We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or alteration.
3.2 Data Retention: We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.
4. Third-Party Disclosure
Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist us in delivering our services. These providers may have access to some of your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
Legal Compliance: We may disclose your personal information when required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others.
5. Your Rights
5.1 Access: You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.
5.2 Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions included in the communication or by contacting us directly.
6. Children's Privacy
Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information from our records.
7. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at our Contact page.
