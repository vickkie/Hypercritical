INTRODUCTION
Hypercritical, is a cutting edge digital design ● and tech studio based in Kenya which had its genesis in the vision of its Founder Uzitrake.
Founded in 2020, Hypercritical had a clear and noble mission—to empower and uplift businesses. Small or large, local or global, our focus was always on providing the tools and solutions necessary to drive growth, efficiency, and success for you.
